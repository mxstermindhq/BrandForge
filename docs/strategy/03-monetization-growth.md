# Monetization & growth strategy

**Purpose:** Turn liquidity and reputation into **sustainable revenue** without killing the competitive, creator-friendly vibe.

## Revenue thesis

1. **Take rate on paid flow** — marketplace match → deal → invoice/checkout is the cleanest lever (align fee % with tier).
2. **Subscriptions / seats** — explorer vs pro vs enterprise for limits (listings, agents, squad size, featured placement).
3. **Credits & usage** — AI tools, agent runs, premium match/boost (metered, transparent).
4. **Seasons & prizes** — sponsor pools and USDT targets as marketing; fund from % of fees or partnerships long-term.

## Pricing posture (strategic)


| Tier                | Job-to-be-done                                                                             |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Free / Explorer** | Get listed, bid, message, complete first deals; hooks to upgrade on limits.                |
| **Pro**             | Volume sellers + buyers: more listings, boosts, lower effective fee or priority placement. |
| **Enterprise**      | Teams, compliance, SSO (later), contract routing—only when pipeline proves pull.           |


**Rule:** fees and limits stay **predictable**—surprise charges destroy trust in B2B marketplaces.

## Growth loops


| Loop          | Mechanism                                                                              |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Supply**    | Public profiles by default + marketplace SEO + leaderboard visibility → inbound.       |
| **Demand**    | Requests, bids, squads → specialists bring buyers; seasonal leaderboard for headlines. |
| **Retention** | Deal rooms + Honor/Conquest + streaks → reason to return weekly.                       |
| **Referral**  | Share `/p/username` and deal outcomes; future formal referral credits.                 |


## GTM (near-term)

1. **Category wedge** — own “AI-era professional deals” narrative on landing + marketplace (consistent story vs generic freelance sites).
2. **Creator/specialist outreach** — templates: list service, join squad, climb leaderboard.
3. **Case studies** — first 10 successful deals documented (anonymized) for landing credibility.
4. **Email** — `hello@brandforge.gg` as single front door; automate onboarding drips when welcome incomplete.

## Metrics to instrument

- **GMV proxy:** sum of accepted bid/offer amounts (even before full escrow).
- **Take rate realized:** % of GMV where platform fee applied.
- **Conversion funnel:** signup → welcome complete → first listing or bid → first message in deal room.
- **CAC placeholder** — track time spent per channel once paid ads start.

## What *not* to do early

- Over-building enterprise SSO before liquidity.
- Hiding fees in FX/crypto layers without disclosure.
- Cannibalizing trust with dark-pattern upsells in chat.
