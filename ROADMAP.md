### Technical Risks
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Scaling issues | Phase 6 dedicated to infrastructure |
| AI costs | Usage limits, caching, model optimization |
| Security breaches | Regular audits, penetration testing |

### Business Risks
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Competition | AI differentiation, community moat |

| Revenue delays | Multiple revenue streams, not just fees |

### Market Risks
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Economic downturn | Focus on ROI for users |

| Regulation | Compliance-first design |
